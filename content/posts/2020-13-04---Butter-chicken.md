## Butter chicken (Chicken Makhani)

- Chicken thighs (boneless 500g)
- Salted Butter
- 1 large onion (finely chopped)
- 3 large cloves garlic
- Ginger
- 2 finger chillies
- Red chilli powder
- Garam masala
- Coriander powder
- Cumin powder
- Salt
- Double cream
- Kasthuri Methi
- Tomato puree
- Cardamom (5)
- Cloves (5)
- Bay leaf (2)
- Cinnamon bark (1)
- Honey 2 teaspoons
- Coriander
